What is an Explainer Video?

In the easiest way - An explainer video is a simple video which breaks down a topic in the most understandable form to help you out from an issue.

The main content of the explainer videos is a mixture of storytelling and brand promotion. It helps you to learn the new elements and concepts step by step and helps to gain knowledge of the subject.

In business terms, explainer videos are also called presentation videos, or visual explanation videos.

Now let’s grasp the method of making an explainer video step-by-step:-

  • Script: Engaging content is ruling social media because it has been backed by a strong script. A well-written script is essential for a successful explainer video. It is important to know the main message that needs to be conveyed to the audience.
  • Concise and Short length: The videos need to be short. Bond (2008) describes this fact based on his research (a project including more than 1,000 participants). He learned that “short videos of 1 to 4 minutes are ideal” and points out that “short 3- or 4-minute YouTube videos help an instructor to make a key point without having to sacrifice a significant amount of time.
  • Focus: To keep a video short and to explain the core message to the target group, it is necessary to keep the content as simple as possible. In the first step, it is important to focus on the “why” rather than on “what” or the “how”.
  • Storytelling: It is a fundamental and powerful element of explainer videos. It is the most effective way to engage and persuade an audience as people’s brains become activated when a good story is told.
  • Visual: Graphics can have an immense effect on the audience and can captivate their imagination. By using metaphoric imagery, the main points of an explanation can be contextualised, making the core message easier to understand and stick with the audience.

Free Explainer Video Makers Without Watermark

Creating professional-quality explainer videos doesn’t have to be rocket science. Here are some of the best free explainer video makers in 2025 that offer features without watermarks that save you time.

Canva:

Canva’s video editor is user-friendly and comes with a variety of templates for creating explainer videos. It also offers free stock images and animations that can be integrated into your video.

Powtoon:

Known for its animated videos, Powtoon offers a free version that is excellent for creating engaging explainer videos. It allows you to add characters, animations, and text and even has templates to get you started

Renderforest:

Another great option for creating explainer videos without a watermark. Render Forest offers free templates, animations, and stock music, making the video creation process simple.

Animaker

A platform designed specifically for beginners, offers free-to-use animated video templates that are perfect for explainer videos. You can also export videos without watermarks.
