Exploring Generative AI Trends and Tools at Devout Growth

  • Devout Growth
  • May 13, 2024

Generative AI is an artificial intelligence technology that can produce various types of content, including text, imagery, audio and synthetic data. The recent buzz around generative AI has been highly driven by the simplicity of new user interfaces for creating high-quality text, graphics and videos in a matter of seconds.

But what do you think, what actually matters? Work being done in seconds or work being done in the most organic and suitable according to your target audience? Well, the answer to this question needs to be detailed as the digital world itself has content artists who feel discouraged due to the invention of AI tools and trends in today’s generation.

THE DEPLETION OF ORGANIC CREATIVITY

There are innumerable people with uncountable ideations and creativity walking our planet in this generation of Artificial Intelligence. Humans are born with talents, talents that need to be enhanced.

No, I am not saying they are all born for the creative world but Yes, they are born with some or the other talents that are in built because that is the beauty of being a human. Its just that the talents they are born with needs to be flourished and to be pushed in order to bring beauty in their imperfections. There are people who actually work on their talents, explore the unseen part of themselves and turn out to be the best version of themselves which actually helps them in knowing themselves, building self confidence, developing the brain power and what not!

But good morning, to the world of Generative AI! where people are getting highly dependant on the quick and professional outlay of everything they are supposed to think of. Hard work and memory utilization is being killed as AI is nothing but a lavish dish decorated and served on your table! The youth of today’s generation are actually the spoon feeding spoilt brats by the AI because they literally depend on the technology for everything.

AI, A BOON OR A BANE?

Staying out of the world of creativity and developing organics in human, Generative AI is no doubt the most advanced technological gift for the generation! Talking about it’s benefits we do have various points to put it on front such as :

  • Reduction in human error : It significantly reduce errors and increase accuracy and precision.
  • Zero risks : It can overcome many risks by letting Generative AI tools and trends do them for us. Whether it be defusing bombs, going to space, exploring the deepest part of ocean or surviving any unfriendly atmosphere. AI tools and trends have literally made life very easy for all of us.
  • Hampering of personal life is not in their command, so there is no tiredness or space of feeling fatigue : As human can only work and should only work at a certain amount of time, AI makes it possible to be working endlessly without a break.
  • Digital Assistance and New Inventions : Some of the most technologically advanced companies engage with users using digital assistants, which eliminates the need for human personnel. Many website utilize digital assistants to deliver user-requested content. Some chatbots are built in a way that makes it difficult to tell whether we are conversing with a human or chatbot. In practically every field, it is the driving force behind numerous innovations that will aid humans in resolving the majority of challenging issues.
  • Daily Applications : It is a master of every applications being used around you. There are various advantages of AI, if we start to talk about the technological or digital world.

    • The fear of omnipotent machines acting like humans isn’t far-fetched. Elon Musk has repeatedly warned that AI will soon become just as smart as humans, and that will be a scary situation because humanity’s existence will be at stake.

    But all this has nothing to do with the creative world, the world of creativity here is far away from how technology puts it up to be. AI can never be a boon for creativity because it makes everything technological. The people who could have used their own will, energy, ideas and mind on any creative topic now feels easy to go through the very approachable and advanced tools and trends of Generative AI . They don’t have energy to work and explore the amazing side of their creative mind which brings out something beautiful, rather, they bring out psychological excuses which ultimately convinces themselves and others.

    GENERATIVE AI TOOLS AND TRENDS WILL ALWAYS BE LESS FOR THE WORLD OF CREATIVITY!!
