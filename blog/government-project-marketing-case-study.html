How we increase 100% business growth in 11 months

  • Devout Growth

Skills are the currency of the future world. How does it feel when your talent is recognized at the right place & time? Felt Joyful!

In the same way, businesses can boom in the market with their competitive strategies if they are marketed well to their target audience. Advertising agencies work as a backbone for business growth and development.

We have worked with various clients, and one of our major government project is BSDM (Bihar Skill Development Mission). We have enhanced their social media platform and increase their traffic from its inception. Before revealing the traffic growth and strategy let’s first explore the nature of the project and the core challenges faced by BSDM and how we as a marketing agency increase their 100% business growth in 11 months.

Client Overview

Bihar Skill Development Mission (BSDM) has been constituted by “The Government of Bihar” to empower the youth by providing them with the requisite skills to fuel the growth of the State of Bihar.

Established in 2010, BSDM's core mission is to provide a skilled workforce by offering vocational training programs in collaboration with various training partners across the state.

Nature Of the Business

They provide a variety of courses based on different skill sets, demonstrate periodic assessments, and dispense certificates of completion with assured employment based on performance. Main objective:-

  • To establish a wide network of training centers for youth
  • To provide employment opportunities to the youth

What’s the main challenge of Government Project (Bsdm)

They emerged as a good initiative but like other business they lacked their presence in the marketplace. However, as digital platforms became a primary source for learning and engagement, BSDM faced several challenges that were hindering its outreach and the impact it could have on the community. And they know very well that their strong digital presence was very crucial to achieve its objectives.

Adventure comes with hurdles, so we had the biggest bombs - competitors and conversions. We have managed all those stumping blocks-

During the first month of analyzing the platform, website, and digital portals of BSDM, we found low traffic, which translated to fewer enrollments in the training programs. Apart from that, we noticed that the platform has a non-interactive feed and an outdated interface, which affected the visibility of the audience.

Tackling these giant rocks was an insightful experience for us.

Objectives:-

Recognize these things firstly we made a good strategy where our main goal is to fix all these issues including social media optimization, website redesigning, competitor analysis, creating accounts on different social media platforms, etc.

As we already seems our client's first goal is to make a presence in the various online platform. Also, we integrated data analytics tools to track user behavior, campaign performance, and engagement to make informed decisions.

Strategic Approach

Do you know, you prepare strategies every single day? Yes, from scheduling the tasks, prioritizing them, and achieving them through those little actions accomplish your daily objectives.

We incorporate the same from scheduling to accomplishing, customized every strategy from in-depth analysis.

After fixing all the issues we move on to our second strategy where our main goal is to prepare a custom strategy for all the platforms like social media, SEO, and marketing. we can say in our first strategy we worked on fixing the issue which had already available on the website or business.

But the second part was totally different here our main focus was to plan for content, in that strategy we focous on these details like seo audit, competitor gap analysis, Semantic seo analysis, off page seo, Q&A sessions, creative visual for social media etc.

Execution

Phase 1: SEO and Content Revamp (Months 1-3)

SEO Audit and Optimization: The first step was a thorough SEO audit. We identified broken links, missing alt texts, and poor keyword usage. Once our audit was complete, an optimization plan was implemented.

Phase 2: Social Media Campaigns and Outreach (Months 6-8)

Engagement-Driven Campaigns: Social media campaigns were launched, focusing on showcasing success stories and program benefits. Hashtags like #BiharSkills and #EmpowerBihar were introduced, encouraging the customers to share their experiences.
