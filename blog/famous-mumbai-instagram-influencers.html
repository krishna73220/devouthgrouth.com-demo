Top 20 Influencers in Mumbai January 2025

  • Devout Growth
  • january 18, 2025

Social media influencers have experienced a phenomenal boom in the past few years and have become an essential channel for communicating with marketers. Mumbai, the city of dreams, is home to a plethora of individuals who have made names for themselves in the world of influencers.

From lifestyle to travel and food vlogs to fashion, this kind of content is on the feed of everyone's scroll. Now, social media has transcended from likes and comments. This becomes financial support to Influencers.

We introduce here the top 20 influencers in Mumbai who are ruling on social media massively-

Best influencers in Mumbai

1. Awez Darbar

Awez Darbar is a social media influencer, dancer, and choreographer. Awez's content style is a perfect blend of dance, entertainment, and lifestyle. If you are active on social media, you may have come across his videos. He has already worked with several Bollywood celebrities like Varun Dhawan, Shraddha Kapoor, and Tiger Shroff. His dance covers and comedy videos are always a treat to watch.

2. Komal Pandey

A popular fashion blogger and influencer, Komal Pandey is known for her impeccable style and beauty tips. Her Instagram feed is a must-follow for anyone who loves fashion and beauty. In 2018, she was listed as the top fashion creator and fashion icon of India. She is known for her bold style and jewelry fashion. Her videos often cross 1 million views. Today, she is one of the most popular influencers in Mumbai. She regularly works with legacy brands like Olay and Amazon.

3. Jannat Zubair Rahmani

Jannat Zubair is one of the popular influencers in Mumbai who creates content around trends, lifestyle, travel, fashion, and style. She started her career through acting, her Instagram feed is a treat to watch that is a collaboration with other influencers and with mostly her brother. She also collaborates with various brands.

4. FAISAL SHAIKH

Faisal Shaikh, popularly known as Faisu, is a social media influencer and content creator. His rise to fame began when he started posting videos on TikTok and Instagram. His lip-sync videos, dance performances, and comedy skits quickly gained him a massive following. He is quite famous among youth, whether he's acting in Bollywood films, creating more hilarious content, or collaborating with other stars.

5. Ashish Chanchlani

There is hardly anyone who does not know this name in the influencers landscape. His content captivated the audience for a long time, as his comedy skits, whether student-teacher skits or any other video, made him an internet sensation at the time. His content creation is unique and humorous. He started with a YouTube channel, and now many Bollywood stars collaborate with him to promote their movies.

6. Kritika Khurana

Do you need some excitement in your life? Look no further than Kritika Khurana's Instagram feed, which is a veritable gold mine of captivating travel, fashion, and lifestyle content! Kritika's path demonstrates the strength of accepting and loving oneself. She shares her experiences and advice that encourage and uplift her audience on her blog, "The Boho Girl," and her podcast, "Whatisupsister," which she co-hosts with her sister Deeksha Khurana.

7. Aashna Shroff

She is another popular influencer in Mumbai known for her chic style. Her Instagram feed is a must-follow for anyone interested in fashion, style, and lifestyle. She was recently awarded Cosmopolitan Luxury Fashion Influencer of the Year 2024. She frequently showcases her unique outfits and collaborates with brands like Dior.

8. Prajakta Koli

Prajakta Koli started with relatable content that used to be new for the audience being feel related. She has a massive fan following across the country and emerged as an actor and host over time. Her presence on social media platforms has been engaging, creative content around topics like mental health, body positivity, and feminism.

Prajakta often collaborates with other Indian YouTubers and influencers to promote the Indian creator community.

9. Shreya Kalra

Shreya Kalra is well-known on social media for creating funny and captivating reels and shorts. She is well-liked in the influencer community because of her charming personality, which has won over millions of hearts. In addition to her skill on social media, Shreya has demonstrated her acting abilities in web series such as Heartbeats.

10. Sanjana Batra

A fashion and lifestyle influencer, Sanjana Batra is popular for her elegant style and love for travel. She is also a renowned stylist in the entertainment industry of Mumbai. She was recently awarded as Cosmopolitan Luxury Fashion Influencer of 2024. The makeup products and dresses are amazing videos, you can take some ideas for yourself.

11. Shayan Siddiqui

The best influencer of men's grooming, lifestyle topics, and hookah culture, sharing insights and trends in these areas. He often travels out of the country, shares videos, and collaborates with other influencers.

12. Malini Agarwal

Malini Agarwal is a public figure who is popular for fashion, podcast, and lifestyle content. She is popularly known as Miss Malini and, surprisingly, author, and she wrote books like "To the Moon: How I Blogged My Way to Bollywood" and "Under the Influence- How to Survive and Thrive Online." Miss Malini has 194k followers and has led the most significant shows, such as "The Good Creator Shows," and has their show "Miss Malini World," where she hosts people like Kareena Kapoor. She has founded "missmalini.komi.io."

13. Masoom Minawala

A popular fashion and lifestyle influencer, Masoom Minawala is known for her chic style and love for beauty products. She is a creator, investor, and entrepreneur. Her ethnic style is mesmerizing. She creates content on lifestyle; recently, she posted about her baby and has also done paid partnerships with brands like Dior, Laurent, and Vogue.

14. Sejal Kumar

YouTube personality and lifestyle influencer is well-known for her travel, beauty, and fashion posts. Sejal has amassed a following of young women who look up to her on Instagram, with over 822 K followers.

Sejal's content style is the ideal fusion of lifestyle, beauty, and fashion. She has worked with well-known companies such as Maybelline, Sephora, and H&M and collaborated with several Indian fashion designers, such as Rohit Bal and Manish Malhotra.

With more than 1.5 million subscribers, Sejal is well-known on YouTube. She posts videos on her YouTube account, such as trip vlogs, beauty tips, and fashion finds.

15. Aisha Ahmed

Aisha Ahmed is a fashion and lifestyle influencer who shares with her fans her travel experiences, favorite cosmetics, and personal style. She also appears in music videos and online series like FilterCopy.

Aisha has worked with several well-known companies, such as MAC Cosmetics, Zara, and H&M. She has also collaborated with several Indian fashion designers, such as Anita Dongre and Sabyasachi Mukherjee.

In addition to blogging, Aisha writes about lifestyle, beauty, and fashion. Her blog offers a variety of topics, such as travel tours, cosmetic lessons, and fashion advice.

16. Chinki Minki

If you remember the line "main chinki aur ye minki," the confusion that twins create among all of them is pretty enjoyable and adorable. The television shows and podcasts invite them to add humor to the show. They also appear in music videos and dramas, along with content creators. The Twin has massive fan-following not only in Mumbai but also in the whole country, where the entire country enjoys its content.

17. Sukhneet Wadhwa

Sukhneet Wadhwa is a lifestyle influencer and YouTube personality known for her fashion, beauty, and travel content.

She has collaborated with several popular brands, including Sephora, H&M, and Maybelline, and has also worked with several Indian fashion designers, including Manish Malhotra and Rohit Bal.

She has a strong presence on YouTube, which features a wide range of content, including fashion hauls, beauty tutorials, and travel vlogs.

18. Karishma Govil

Meet Karishma Govil, popularly known as Soul Kari, a digital influencer who has captured the hearts of millions with her engaging content. Her Instagram feed is a treasure trove of comedy, travel, and lifestyle videos that are sure to leave you smiling.

One of the highlights is the #SunoMehrBano series, featuring an irresistibly cute kid who will melt your heart with their innocence and charm.

She is also the founder and CEO of @clapclapindia, where she helps individuals build their social media presence and personality, which will help them thrive on different social media.

19. Karishma (rjkarishma)

The most relatable video of a household with different characters is quite entertaining and impressive to watch. The kid character, mom things, Indian shadi drama, and sibling fight that she played are quite engaging to the audience. Her videos are quite sarcastic and give funny vibes. She worked as a radio jockey at Red FM.

20. Abhinav Mathur

He is known as a men's fashion and luxury lifestyle influencer. Luxury Content Creator also awarded him of the years 24-25. His innovative approach to content and storytelling is enough to captivate an audience. The content he creates of big-budget stuff attracts the audience.

Conclusion-

Now, we know the influencers world is not just for fun but the way we can boost our social presence. From fashion and beauty to lifestyle and entertainment, these influencers have built massive followings and are redefining the world of social media marketing. These include content creation, creativity, and a relatable and engaging vibe that can attract users.
