Top 10 food influencers in india January 2025

  • Devout Growth
  • january 16, 2025

India has always been a hub of rich flavors and exotic spices, captivating the world with its culinary heritage, diverse traditions, and age-old recipes that tell stories of culture and history. Food influencers have become integral to the culinary world, sharing mouth-watering recipes, honest food reviews, and immersive dining experiences that inspire millions of food enthusiasts worldwide. From exciting food challenges and efficient cooking hacks to healthy snacking tips and dietary recipes, food bloggers cover it all, making the culinary world more accessible, inspiring, and delicious for their audiences.

India's top food bloggers share their culinary knowledge and personal experiences, empowering their audiences to recreate delicious dishes effortlessly from the comfort of their kitchens.

In this blog, we will explore some of the top food influencers in India who have significantly impacted the culinary scene with their delectable content, inspiring food journeys, and flavorful creations.

Top Food Influencers in India 2025

1. Shivesh Bhatia

Shivesh Bhatia is a renowned self-taught baker, author, and digital creator from Delhi, India. His journey from home baker to International recognition has been inspiring. He is known for his visually stunning desserts and easy-to-follow recipes. Shivesh has created an impressive career through his blog, "Bake with Shivesh." His remarkable talent and hard work have earned him numerous awards over the years.

When he was just 22 years old, he was featured in Vogue India's list of 20 under 26 along with being awarded the Outlook Social Media Award 2016 and was named Social Media Star of the Year by Conde Nast Traveller India; in addition to this, Better homes and gardens recognized best desert blog 2017.

In 2018, Shivesh was awarded Best Insta Grammar of the Year by Living Foods. His creative content journey continues to shine, which led to the title of Best Food Feed of the Year at the Cosmopolitan Blogger Awards in 2019, 2021, and 2022; he also got featured in Vogue India's 30 under 30 list in 2023

Shivesh journey exemplifies how passion, creativity, and dedication can turn a hobby into a thriving career.

2. Nisha Madhulika

Nisha Madhulika is one of India's most loved YouTube food bloggers. A teacher turned Into YouTuber, who is now the richest female YouTuber in India, has a net worth of 43 cr at the age of 65.

With 14.5 million followers on YouTube, she started creating content as an alternative career and started making easy and simple recipes that connect with people.

Ghar Ka Khana, a famous channel in the country, is loved for its sound and heartwarming vegetarian recipes, which have made Nisha Madhulika a household name. Her contribution to popularizing home-style Indian cuisine has earned her several prestigious awards, including the Padma Shri Award in 2021, along with features in Forbes India, BBC, and other prominent media outlets. She has also been honored with the Social Media Star Award for influencing the culinary world.

Today, Ghar Ka Khana is not just a phrase but a feeling Nisha Madhulika has brought into countless homes, making her a symbol of comfort, tradition, and culinary excellence. Her journey is a true testament to the fact that age is just a number.

3. Pooja Dhingra

Pooja Dhingra is celebrated as the pastry chef and an entrepreneur, founder of India's first Macron store, and created the bakery chain Le15 Patisserie, known for its exquisite macarons, cupcakes, and other French-inspired treats.

Earlier, she was a student of law, but slowly, she realized her passion for bakery; she studied at Le Cordon Bleu, Paris, where she practiced the arts of French pastry-making; returning to India in 2010, she introduced the country to authentic Parisian-style macarons, making Le15 Patisserie, a household name among desserts lovers.

She was featured on the Forbes 30 under 30. She continues to inspire millions of women through podcasts, workshops, and social media., and she tirelessly works for women entrepreneurs; her story is a testament to following one's passion and redlining the pastry scene in India.

4. Vikas Khanna

Vikas Khanna's inspiring journey from Amritsar, India, to New York is a tale of true passion, perseverance, and hard work. On 10 December 2024, his restaurant bungalow was honored with the Michelin 2024 Bib Gourmand Award, which stands as a testament to his hard work and highlights his high-quality food at affordable prices, merging with world-class flavors with a welcoming and accessible dining experience

As a judge on MasterChef India, his visionary mentorship on the show goes beyond teaching recipes. It's more about nurturing creativity and encouraging the young chefs to push boundaries.

5. Ranveer Brar

From becoming the youngest executive chef at just 25 to emerging as one of India’s finest and most celebrated chefs, Ranveer Brar has carved a remarkable journey in the culinary world. A celebrated author, actor, and culinary expert, his journey showcases his multifaceted talent.

What truly sets him apart is his unique style of storytelling, where he weaves history and culture into the food he creates. He has been featured among the Top 50 chefs in the Indian Culinary Forum book.

His ability to bring the stories behind traditional dishes to life is nothing short of captivating, leaving an indelible impact on food lovers everywhere.

6. Kunal Kapur

Kunal Kapur is a celebrity chef known for hosting and judging MasterChef India. He is also a restaurateur, author, and media personality. Chef Kunal has stolen the hearts of millions of food enthusiasts nationwide.

His unique approach to cooking and innovative recipes have helped him become a household name.

7. Saloni kukreja

A food explorer in search of the essence of cuisine around the globe, Saloni Kukreja is a passionate traveler and food blogger. She began her food blogging journey three years ago with a mission to discover hidden flavors and diverse cooking styles.

Her blog, ‘Food of Mumbai,’ showcases a blend of the best recipes from across the city. Her unique approach to content creation attracts audiences from all over the nation.

8. Harpal Singh Sohki

The blend of laughter, a unique sense of humor, and an exceptional cooking style define Chef Harpal Singh Sokhi, the host of the popular cooking show Turban Tadka. Known for his talent in cooking and comedy, he is fondly referred to as the Laughter Chef.

Chef Harpal Singh presents Indian cuisine with an intelligent balance of flavors and spices through his YouTube channel, which is gaining popularity in every Indian kitchen. He continues to add “Namak Shamak” to recipes while spreading joy through his charismatic cooking style.

9. Uma Raghuraman

Masterchefmom, known as Uma Raghuraman, is a passionate chef who began her culinary journey decades ago. She is renowned for her vegetarian recipes shared on her blog Masterchef Mom, where she features tried-and-tested, home-flavor-loaded recipes that are easy to make.

Uma aims to inspire her audience to embrace regional South Indian vegetarian cuisine through her creative and authentic recipes.

Her exceptional culinary content has earned her numerous accolades. At the prestigious 2017 SAVEUR Blog Awards, in a glittering ceremony in Charleston, SC, she won the Best Food Instagram Award.

In 2020, she received the LLEGA Best Indian Food Micro Blogger Award. In 2022, Forbes India and INCA recognized her as one of India’s Top 100 Digital Stars, celebrating her significant influence in the digital culinary space.

10. Sanjyot Kheer

Sanjyot Keer is an Indian chef and emerging internet personality who has worked with well-known chefs across the nation.

He is recognized for giving a creative twist to Indian comfort food. Chef Sanjyot Keer is the face behind Your Food Lab, which has risen to become one of the most renowned food blogs and Instagram pages.

His passion for creating delicious and visually stunning dishes has captured the hearts of food enthusiasts nationwide.

Conclusion

In this realm, people easily connect through food, which bridges cultural gaps despite geographical barriers, and food brings people closer.

Food Instagrammers have proven this right with their unique and creative recipes that make your mouth water, making it hard to resist trying these recipes in the comfort of your home. Their entertaining storytelling and historical insights about food will leave you awestruck.
