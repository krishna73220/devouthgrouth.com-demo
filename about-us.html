About Us

Two individuals from Bihar. One shaping stories in a national newsroom. The other coding futures in a U.S. tech giant.

Their roots were common. So was their concern: “In this age of digital dominance, can Bihar’s traditional businesses survive without modern marketing?” And if not- can they afford MNC-level agencies?

The answer was ‘No.’ But that ‘No’ sparked a revolution.

They didn’t want to follow the system. They wanted to build a new one. And from this bold thinking came a line that defined everything: “Jahaan log chappal nahi pehente the, humne wahan logo ko joota pehnane ki sochi.” Not just shoes- but the confidence to walk into the digital future. That thought became Devout Growth Media Pvt. Ltd. What started from a one-room setup with 7 dreamers, grew to a powerhouse of 150+ professionals. From Patna to Bengaluru, and soon in Delhi and Gurgaon - our reach reflects our ambition.
